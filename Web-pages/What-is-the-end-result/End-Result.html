What does that even mean?(What is the end result?)

Ultimately, we will have a decentralized, peer-to-peer system that forms a symmetrical manifold, where the dynamics of its nodes are governed by fluid dynamics. For each node, we begin with a Gaussian white noise model and define the convolution computation, as well as the noise addition and removal processes, as a node rotation based on an elliptical function.
